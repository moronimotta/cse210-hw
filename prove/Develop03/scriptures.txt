Scripture: John 3:16
Content: For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.
Hint: This verse is often quoted to summarize the central message of Christianity.

Scripture: Proverbs 3:5-6
Content: Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight.
Hint: This verse encourages you to rely on God for guidance in all aspects of life.

Scripture: Psalm 23:1
Content: The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing.
Hint: This verse begins with a declaration of trust in God's care and provision.

Scripture: Alma 32:21
Content: And now as I said concerning faith - faith is not to have a perfect knowledge of things; therefore, if ye have faith ye hope for things which are not seen, which are true.
Hint: This verse discusses the concept of faith and its relationship to hope.

Scripture: Moroni 10:3-5
Content: Behold, I would exhort you that when ye shall read these things, if it be wisdom in God that ye should read them, that ye would remember how merciful the Lord hath been unto the children of men, from the creation of Adam even down until the time that ye shall receive these things, and ponder it in your hearts.
Hint: This passage encourages reflection on God's mercy and the importance of pondering spiritual truths.
